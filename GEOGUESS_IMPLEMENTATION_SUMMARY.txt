🎮 HOW IT WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CREATING A CHALLENGE:
  1. Main Menu → GeoGuess → Create Challenge
  2. Navigate world (search/minimap)
  3. Click "Add" → Click on map to place flag
  4. Edit label & hint
  5. Repeat for more locations
  6. Fill challenge info (name, category, etc.)
  7. Preview or Save

PLAYING A CHALLENGE:
  1. Main Menu → GeoGuess → Play Challenge
  2. Select challenge from list
  3. Spawns at each location (altitude limited)
  4. Explore with aircraft or free camera
  5. Open guessing map
  6. Click to place guess → Confirm
  7. See results (distance, points)
  8. Continues to next location
  9. Final score & best score saved
